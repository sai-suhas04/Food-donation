How to Donate Food



Browse Food Donation Options:

1. Visit the homepage and navigate to the "Donate Food" section.

2. Browse through the list of accepted food items and categories.


Select Donation Type:

1. Choose between a one-time donation or setting up recurring donations.

2. Indicate the quantity and type of food items you wish to donate.


Fill Out Donation Form:

1. Provide your contact information for communication and coordination.

2. Select your preferred pickup date and time, if applicable.


Review and Confirm:

1. Double-check your donation details, including food items and pickup information.

2. Confirm your donation and proceed to the next steps.
